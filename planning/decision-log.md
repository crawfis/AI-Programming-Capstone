# Decision Log

*Append-only. Every major decision gets an entry below. The kickoff skill creates the first entry.*

Use this log to capture your learning journey. Every time you make a significant decision — choosing a framework, cutting a feature, pivoting your approach — add an entry here.

### Entry Format

```
## [Date] — [Decision Title]

**Context:** What prompted this decision?
**Decision:** What did you decide?
**Alternatives considered:** What else did you consider?
**Rationale:** Why this choice?
**Frameworks applied:** (if any — SWOT, MoSCoW, RICE, etc.)
**Documents updated:** Which planning docs changed as a result?
**What I learned:** What did this teach you about your project or process?
```

See [Decision Frameworks](../docs/decision-frameworks.md) for frameworks you can apply.
